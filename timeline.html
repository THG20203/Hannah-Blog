2014
I quit my job, bought a ticket and travelled to the other side of the world to live and work on a dairy farm in New Zealand.
2015
I returned home after 7 months away but still couldn't settle so continued my agricultural work experience in West Wales and Scotland (seasonally lambing for a number of years). Got myself a new job working in one section of the agricultural industry as well as starting an Agricultural degree part time.
2017
Changed jobs but remained within the agricultural industry. A job which I could work with farmers as well as broaden my knowledge on UK farming systems and where I am still working today.
2018
Completed and gained a Foundation degree in Agriculture and decided to top it up to a full degree over a couple more years, all whilst working full time. I purchased my own small flock of sheep to start putting into practice what I had been learning since 2014.
2019
Got to travel to New Zealand again and visit numerous farming businesses on a three week tour.
2020
Completed my Bachelor of Science with Honours in Agriculture with a First Class Honours.
