Reasons for doing this....

The silent heroes of the land. The strong, decisive, caring and resilient. The mothers, daughters, sisters and friends. They are the women of the land.

Growing up, I have fond memories of many women living and working within my rural community and as a young woman I found myself being surrounded by these loving, determined and family orientated ladies. I was always amazed by the contributions they brought to their homes, businesses and rural landscapes.
